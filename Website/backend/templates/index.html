What is Retro?

Retro enables patients’ pertinent medical histories and information to be accessed, on-demand, through scanning a QR code — this information can potentially save lives in emergency situations while also increasing medical data's accessibility.

Our Features.

Easy Access

Medical information stored in Retro can be accessed by quickly scanning a QR code, a feature built into almost all modern smartphones.

Medical Insights

Retro organizes information into a clean and intuitive format that is useful for not only emergency response situations but also for general health insights.

Data Availability

Users have full access to their data at all times, regardless of the specific medical professionals or doctors that they regularly visit.
